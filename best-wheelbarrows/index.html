A wheelbarrow or garden cart is one of those tools you don’t think about until you have to haul mulch across the yard or move a pile of stones. Get it right, and you save your back and your time. The key choices come down to capacity (how much volume you typically carry), wheel type (a single pneumatic wheel handles rough ground better, while two wheels offer more stability on pavement), and material (poly carts won’t rust but can crack; steel trays handle sharp, heavy loads). We spent weeks researching owner experiences, expert reviews, and dealer feedback to find the models that hold up year after year.
